# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Windows productivity users who need glanceable, non-interruptive controls for ongoing work at the top center of the desktop.

## Product Purpose

Surface the single most relevant activity without taking users out of flow. Expansion should reveal a calm overview first, then progressively disclose focused tools such as the Time Center.

## Brand Personality

Restrained, precise, lightweight. Familiar Windows ergonomics meet the interaction discipline of Apple Dynamic Island and prototype B's compact rail with flat activity rows.

## Anti-references

The rejected dense dashboard: nested cards, permanent forms, rows of equal-weight buttons, and management controls competing on the home surface.

## Design Principles

- Glance first; management second.
- One primary action per activity.
- Progressive disclosure over permanent controls.
- Preserve spatial continuity across collapsed, hover, and expanded states.
- Use native capability quietly; never let technical chrome dominate the task.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA contrast, preserve keyboard navigation and visible focus, respect reduced motion, and provide a high-contrast fallback without relying on color alone.
